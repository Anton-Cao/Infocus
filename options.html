About

  • Add websites that distract you to the extension
  • When you visit a blocked site, the extension will close the tab or go back to the previous site for you!
  • Use the shortcut ctrl+i (command+i on Mac) to enable Infocus
  • You have to solve a few math problems to disable Infocus :)
  • Optionally set the timer for how long you want to work - Infocus will automatically turn off afterwards
  • When disabled, Infocus will periodically ask you to focus again if you are on a distracting site
